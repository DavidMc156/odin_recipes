Ingredients

For the meat sauce

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 celery sticks, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot (approx 100g), finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, finey chopped
  • 140g cubetti di pancetta
  • 500g beef mince
  • 500g pork mince or british veal mince
  • 200ml milk
  • 2 x 400g tins chopped tomatoes
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 rosemary sprig
  • 2 thyme sprigs
  • 2 beef stock cubes
  • 500ml red wine

For the lasagne

  • about 400g dried pasta sheets
  • 50g parmesan, finely grated

For the white sauce (bechamel)

  • 1.5l milk
  • 1 onion
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 3 cloves
  • 100g butter
  • 100g plain flour
  • good grating of nutmeg
